About Bella Vista

Our Story

Bella Vista comes from two Italian words meaning “Beautiful View.” The name was inspired by the idea that every guest deserves more than just a place to stay or dine — they deserve a beautiful experience.
Although Bella Vista is an Italian phrase, its meaning fits beautifully into Indian culture. In ancient Indian traditions, every place of hospitality — from royal guest houses to community inns — was designed to provide a “shubh drishti,” meaning an auspicious and delightful view.
